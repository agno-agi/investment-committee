# Alphabet Inc. (GOOGL)

## Business Overview
Alphabet is the parent company of Google, the world's dominant search engine and digital advertising platform. The company also operates YouTube, Google Cloud, Waymo (autonomous vehicles), and various "Other Bets" including Verily (healthcare) and Wing (drone delivery).

## Revenue Segments
- **Google Search & Other:** Search advertising (~57% of revenue). Google maintains 90%+ global search market share.
- **YouTube Ads:** Video advertising and YouTube Premium/TV subscriptions (~10% of revenue).
- **Google Cloud:** Cloud computing platform, Google Workspace (~12% of revenue). Third-largest cloud provider behind AWS and Azure.
- **Google Network:** Ad revenue from partner websites and apps.
- **Other Bets:** Waymo, Verily, and other ventures. Revenue minimal but potential optionality.

## Competitive Position
Google Search is one of the most dominant monopolies in technology. The company's AI capabilities (DeepMind, Gemini models) are world-class. YouTube has no true competitor at scale for long-form video. Google Cloud is the fastest-growing major cloud platform.

## Growth Drivers
- AI integration into Search (AI Overviews) could increase engagement and ad revenue
- Google Cloud growth accelerating with AI workloads and Gemini model adoption
- YouTube monetization expanding (Shorts ads, Premium subscriptions, NFL Sunday Ticket)
- Waymo autonomous ride-hailing expanding to new cities
- Gemini model family competing directly with OpenAI GPT
